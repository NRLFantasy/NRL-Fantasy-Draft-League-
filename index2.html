NRL Fantasy Draft League

League Rules

Official rules and governance of the NRL Fantasy Draft League.

Navigate using the menu above.

Section 1 – League Structure

  • Draft based competition between registered members.
  • Teams must maintain full rosters.
  • Season structure decided before start.
  • Members agree to rules upon joining.
  • Commissioner may adjust settings if required.

Section 2 – Draft Rules

  • Draft order random or agreed.
  • Members must draft within time.
  • Auto draft if time expires.
  • Players belong to drafting team.
  • Commissioner resolves disputes.

Section 3 – Team Management

  • Teams must remain active.
  • Inactive teams may be managed.
  • Lineups set before deadlines.
  • Injured players replaceable.
  • No tanking allowed.

Section 4 – Scoring

  • Points follow fantasy metrics.
  • Weekly matchups decide ladder.
  • Tiebreakers predetermined.
  • Stat corrections applied.
  • Commissioner resolves disputes.

Section 5 – Trades

  • Trades via official system.
  • Trades reviewed if needed.
  • No collusion allowed.
  • Unfair trades may be vetoed.
  • Deadlines enforced.

Section 6 – Free Agency

  • Free agents available equally.
  • Waivers determine priority.
  • Add/drop allowed unless locked.
  • No exploitation allowed.
  • Commissioner intervention possible.

Section 7 – Player Movement

  • Trades must be official.
  • No fake trades via waivers.
  • Classified as collusion.
  • Violations punished.
  • Penalties include removal.

Section 8 – Waivers

  • Priority decides claims.
  • Deadlines enforced.
  • Priority resets after claims.
  • No manipulation allowed.
  • Commissioner decisions final.

Section 9 – League Integrity

  • Members must act fairly.
  • No unfair advantage.
  • No exploiting systems.
  • No harassment.
  • Penalties enforced.

Section 10 – Commissioner Authority

  • Commissioner enforces rules.
  • May interpret unclear rules.
  • Decisions are final.
  • Acts to protect integrity.
  • Authority accepted by members.
